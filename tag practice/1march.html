Sunder Pichai

Biography

Sundar Pichai or Pichai Sundararajan is an Indian Cheif Executive Officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. Sundar Pichai joined Google as a product management executive in 2004 and also helped in developing the Google Chrome Browser. Now he came in the list of world’s highest-paid- CEOs. Sundar Pichai was born on 10 June 1972 in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. His father, Regunatha Pichai, was an electrical engineer at GEC, the British conglomerate, and his mother, Lakhsmi Pichai, was a stenographer. He has a younger brother Srinivasan Pichai.
